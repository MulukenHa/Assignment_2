When comparing the image formats in terms of loading time from lowest to highest, it generally depends on the image content and usage. However, for typical use cases, we can make a generalized ranking based on average file sizes and typical usage scenarios: JPEG: Generally has the fastest loading times for photographs and complex images due to efficient lossy compression resulting in smaller file sizes. GIF: Typically has faster loading times than PNG for simple images or small animations due to limited color palettes and simpler content. PNG: Usually has the slowest loading times, especially for complex images, due to lossless compression and larger file sizes.

    Ranking

  1. JPEG: Lowest loading time
  2. GIF: Moderate loading time
  3. PNG: Highest loading time
